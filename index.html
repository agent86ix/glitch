FavorSaver
FavorSaver is a tool that can analyze your inventory and give you an idea as to what you should donate (and how much) to reach your favor goals.
Skillifier!
Skillifier is a tool that helps you plan your skill learning order, and helps you visualize the impact of Better Learning and the "Brain Full" penalty.
GrindOTron!
Grind-O-Tron is a tool for miners and alchemists that helps you understand powder making and compounds, based on the current contents of your inventory and what you desire to make.
FAQ
Visit the FAQ to get answers to all your questions! As long as your questions are in the FAQ.

About

Thanks for visiting! I'm glad that you find this site and its tools useful.

I'm a professional software developer by day, and in my free time (ie, the five seconds a day I'm not working, taking care of my kids, or playing video games myself) I like to do interesting and fun things with technology.

You may notice a certain 'minamalist charm' about my site, and part of that is that I have very little patience for browsers and CSS quirks, and part of that is my interesting hosting arrangement, wherein I pay only for what I use. Therefore, I've designed this entire site to need very little in the way of server-side code and locally hosted images/scripts.

If you're interested, you can view my profile.

If you have issues, suggestions, or just want to chat, you can join the group here.

These tools work their magic via the Glitch Developer API and jQuery, along with a generous helping of secret sauce that I apply personally.

If you're having an issue, please report it in the group forums or the main glitch forum thread that lead you here. Please let me know what browser and OS you are using. I try to support every commonly available browser, but Opera is missing a key feature that keeps it from working properly.
